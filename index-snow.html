Hello! I am Dek Haji

As long as I remember I always admired building electronic devices, thought my self how to connect components and wires, also studied circuit diagrams and know how to build them. As a kid, I never grew up having computers around me, but when I did, I had more fun building them than I did actually played games on them. two years ago, while I was busy preparing my A+ certification exam I came across CS50 free Computer Science Courses Program from Harvard, after completing the first course and wrote my first HELLO WORLD code, that moment was when I realized how much I enjoyed coding, without hesitating I applied for Nashville Community College with a major of computer information technology, I was disappointed with a lack of hands-on coding experience, I did online courses but still was lacking a communication with real people and connecting like-minded people, after long search and talking to a lot of friends I decided to try NSS to become a Full Stack .NET C# Software Developer. After a competitive application/interview process, finally, I was lucky enough to join the NSS family. and that is the best decision I ever mad. Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life. That's my MOTO .
